Key Projects

SmartDrive AI – Vehicle Safety System

  • Developed a machine learning system to enhance driver safety by detecting signs of fatigue or drowsiness.
  • Implemented mouth movement detection to identify yawning and track eye states for drowsiness analysis.
  • Integrated vehicle control features: the system reduces vehicle speed and issues an alert when drowsiness is detected.
  • Tools and Frameworks: Python, OpenCV, Flask

GlucoseTrack AI – Smart Diabetes Risk Analyzer

  • Designed a predictive system to analyze diabetes risk using machine learning models.
  • Leveraged Random Forest algorithms to classify and predict diabetes outcomes with over 80% accuracy.
  • Enhanced the system with a user-friendly interface for medical data input such as glucose levels, BMI, and age.
  • Tools and Frameworks: Python, Scikit-learn, Pandas, Flask
